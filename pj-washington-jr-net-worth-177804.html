PJ Washington Jr. Net Worth

PJ Washington Jr. is a basketball player from Louisville, KY, born on August 23, 1998. He made his debut as a small forward with the Kentucky Wildcats in 2017, and had previously played in the Jordan Brand Classic and McDonald's All-American game. He has since gone on to have a successful career in the NBA.

About

Basketball player who debuted as a small forward with the Kentucky Wildcats in 2017. He had played in the Jordan Brand Classic and McDonald's All-American game earlier that year. 

Before Fame

He graduated from Findlay Prep in Henderson, Nevada. 

Trivia

He won a gold medal representing the U.S. at the FIBA Americas U18 Championship. 

Family Life

His parents are Sherry and Paul Washington, both of whom played basketball at Middle Tennessee State. He has siblings named Spencer, Tylerand Alexandria. 

Associated With

He has been coached by John Calipari at Kentucky.
