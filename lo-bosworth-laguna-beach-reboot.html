Lo Bosworth Talks 'Laguna Beach' Reboot (EXCLUSIVE)

Lo Bosworth Says a 'Laguna Beach' Reboot Would Be "So Nostalgic in the Best Way" (EXCLUSIVE)

Lo Bosworth was only a teenager when 'Laguna Beach' came out. Now, the show is celebrating its 20th anniversary.

By Pretty Honore

May 29 2024, Published 9:52 a.m. ET

Many may know Lo Bosworth as the innovative mastermind behind Love Wellness, but before she became a badass girl boss, she was one of the original “it” girls of the early 2000s.

Lo was only a teenager when she made her television debut on Laguna Beach in 2004. Lo dished on her time on the show and teased that a reunion may or may not be on the horizon in an exclusive interview with Distractify.

Read on for more!

Lo Bosworth talks the possibility of a 'Laguna Beach' reboot: “I think it would just feel so nostalgic in the best way.”

These days, reality TV is synonymous with shade-throwing and tea-spilling, but that hasn’t always been the case.

According to Lo, Laguna Beach's success can be attributed to the show’s focus on friendship. The entrepreneur said even today, the show holds a special in her heart.

“When people come up to me and ask me about Laguna Beach, they say that they're so impressed by our relationships,” Lo told us. “It was made with kindness, and it wasn't just focused on creating drama and issues for the audience, you know, for viewership.”

Thanks to platforms like Netflix and Paramount Plus, viewers of all ages can tune in to all three seasons of Laguna Beach and relive the magic all over again. Old and new fans alike can rejoice in knowing that a reboot of the series may or may not be on the horizon.

Although Lo and her former castmates keep in touch via phone, she said they haven’t linked up IRL in a “very long time.” However, that might change sooner than later.

“We've been talking about a Laguna Beach reboot actually, because this is the 20th anniversary of the show — also my high school’s 20th anniversary from when we graduated,” Lo revealed. “I think it would be so lovely to get everybody back together.”

Lo gushed: “I think it would just feel so nostalgic in the best way. I think it would be so fun to do.”

Lo will always be a California girl at heart, but today, she’s a big-shot businesswoman living in the Big Apple. And as the owner of an industry-leading feminine care brand, she practices what she preaches.

Lo said that self-care is her number one priority, which is why her partnership with BIC Soleil is totally on-brand.

Lo Bosworth’s partnership with BIC Soleil is a testament to her dedication to self-care.

For Lo, having a self-care routine is essential to slaying the day — and BIC’s Soleil’s 5 Senses Shave kit (available for $59 on BIC.com) will help you do both. The kit comes complete with BIC Soleil Escape’s five-blade scented razor, a shower speaker, a hanging bundle of eucalyptus, and lavender and vanilla mocktails.

In her interview with Distractify, Lo shared how BIC’s 5 Senses Shave kit takes self-care to the next level.

“It doesn't require an hour of exercise, or you know, like no counting carbohydrates for the day, right? Those sort of like classic concepts of, 'Oh, this is how I take care of my body,'” Lo shared.

She went on: “There are really loving ways that you can take care of your body, and I think BIC is doing that really well with the shave kit.”
