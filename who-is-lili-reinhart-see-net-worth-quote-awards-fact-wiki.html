Lili Reinhart Wiki Biography

Lili Pauline Reinhart was born on 13 September 1996, in Cleveland, Ohio USA, and is an actress, best known for being a part of the drama series “Riverdale” in which she plays the role of Betty Cooper. She’s been active in the industry since 2008, and all of her efforts have helped put her net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Lili Reinhart? As of early-2018, sources inform us of a net worth that is at $1 million, mostly earned through a successful career in acting. She’s had several roles in films as well, and has also won several awards during her career. As she continues working, it is expected that her wealth will also continue to increase.

Lili grew up in Bay Village, Ohio, early developing a love for dancing, singing and acting. She tried to convince her mom to drive her to auditions in New York, but at 18 years of age, she left home and moved to Los Angeles to try and pursue a career in acting. After getting nowhere for five months, she nearly gave up, and that’s when the work started to roll in.

After appearing in a few short films, Reinhart made notable guest appearances in several television shows, including as a part of “Law & Order: Special Victims Unit” and “Scientastic!” Soon more opportunities would come her way, which would start to increase her net worth. In 2012, she made one of her first films in “Not Waving But Drowning”, a drama film starring Vanessa Ray.

The following year, Reinhart was then cast as Vicki in “The Kings of Summer”, which is a coming of age film that was showcased during the 2013 Sundance Film Festival. She also appeared in the psychological thriller “Forever’s End”, directed by J. C. Shroder. In 2014, she began the regular role as Heather Blumeyer in the series “Surviving Jack”, which is a sitcom based on the autobiographical book entitled “I Suck at Girls”. Two years later, she then had two films, one of which was “Miss Stevens” , a drama film and the directing debut of Julia Hart. She also appeared in “The Good Neighbor” , a thriller starring James Caan and Logan Miller.

In 2017 Lili got one of her most iconic roles, in the series “Riverdale” in which she plays Betty Cooper. The series is based on the characters of “Archie Comics”, and received good reviews, plus for her role in the series, Lili won two Teen Choice Awards including “Choice Breakout TV Star”.

She’s now set to appear in the film “Galveston”, based on the Nic Pizzolatto novel of the same name.

For her personal life, not much is known about Reinhart’s romantic relationships, if any. She has been open about how “Riverdale” helped her when she was going through depression. She’s also active on social media, having over 5.6 million followers on Instagram and over 1.2 million on Twitter. She still resides in Los Angeles.
