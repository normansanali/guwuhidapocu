Julianne Hough family, husband, children, parents, siblings

The 35-year-old Julianne Alexandra Hough was born on July 20, 1988, in Orem, Utah, United States, and is best known as an American dancer, actress, singer, and television personality. In 2007, she joined the cast of ABC’s Dancing with the Stars as a professional dancer, winning two seasons with her celebrity partners.

Table of Content

Julianne Hough husband

Julianne Hough was married to Brooks Laich. The couple announced their engagement on August 18, 2015, and were married on July 8, 2017. They separated in 2020 but later attempted to reconcile. Hough filed for divorce on November 2, 2020; the divorce was finalized on February 22, 2022.

Julianne Hough children

Julianne Hough is yet to be a mother, but as of now, she has no children being they biological or adopted.

Julianne Hough parents

Julianne Hough is the youngest child of Bruce Robert Hough and Marriann Hough who got divorced in 1998. Her father was twice chairman of the Utah Republican Party and ran for Utah’s 2nd congressional district in 2023.

Julianne Hough siblings

Julianne Hough has four siblings, a brother and three sisters namely Derek Hough, Katherine Hough, Sharee Hough, and Marabeth Hough. Her brother, Derek Hough, is also a professional dancer.
