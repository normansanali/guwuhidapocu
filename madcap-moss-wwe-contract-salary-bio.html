Madcap Moss Avoided Injury On The Nasty Slam In WWE Elimination Chamber| Explore His Salary, Contrac

Madcap Moss managed to put on a show when he wrestled against Drew Mclntyre in a Falls Count Anywhere match at Saturday's WWE Elimination Chamber on 19 February 2022 in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. However, he lost to Crew Mclyntre in the match.

Madcap survived a nasty bump right on his head when Drew performed Alabama Slam on him. Drew simply leaned forward for the slam, and Moss should have landed flat on his stomach with his arms out, but Moss kept his head tucked like he was trying to roll through it, but instead, he landed right directly on his head, causing many people to worry about his head. 

After the performance, he was checked out backstage, and it was confirmed that he didn't suffer any injury in the process. The video clearly showed how hard he landed on his head, so it should be considered as a miracle that he has avoided any injury on the Nasty Slam in WWE Elimination Chamber.

Madcap Moss WWE Contract, Career, & Salary

Madcap Moss was signed to WWE in 2014 after he attended a WWE tryout after contacting Jim Ross. He had trained at the WWE Performance Center in Florida, and his first ring name was Digg Rawls. And with that name, he debuted at an NXT live event in Tampa, Florida, in 2014.

Madcap made his television debut on NXT in 2015, and he mainly performed at house shows. He is mainly known for his tag team work with Tino Sabbatelli from 2016-2018 in NXT and also had good teamwork with new tag team Dorian Mak as part of The Outliers in 2019. He is a two-time winner of the WWE Performance Center Combine Champion and WWE 24-7 Championship. He worked with the WWE NXT brand until 2019 and was called up to the main roster in Jan 2020 and defeated Erik of The Viking Raiders on the 1 October 2020 edition of WWE Main Event. After that, he returned to WWE Television by helping Happy Corbin attack Kevin Owens and had a match against Odyssey Jones on 17 September 2021.

His finishers are Powerslam, and his signature moves are Fallaway Slam and Gori Bomb. His estimated net worth is around $1 million, and he has reportedly earned $200,000 as his basic salary.

Madcap Moss Bio

Madcap Moss has a height of 6 ft 3 inches and weighs 245 lbs. His birth name is Michael Mike Rallis, and he comes from Minneapolis, Minnesota, born on 10 October 1989. His debut date was 18 December 2014, and he had three-ring names before his current one, 'Madcap Moss.' He was first named Digg Rawls, then Mike Rawls and Riddick Moss.

He used to play football before wrestling. He attended the University of Minnesota, a linebacker on the Golden Gophers team.
