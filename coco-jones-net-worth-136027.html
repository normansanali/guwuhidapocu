Coco Jones Net Worth

Coco Jones is an American singer and actress who rose to fame for her role as Roxie in the Disney Channel movie Let It Shine. She was born on January 4, 1998 in Columbia, SC and was featured on Disney's radio program Next Big Thing (N.B.T.). She has since gone on to have a successful career in music and acting.

About

She rose to fame for her role as Roxie on the Disney Channel movie Let It Shine. Disney featured her on the radio program Next Big Thing, or N.B.T. 

Before Fame

She sang "America the Beautiful" at her kindergarten graduation when she was six. 

Trivia

She performed at the NAACP Awards Nominees Luncheon in 2012, and gained attention for her cover of Aretha Franklin's "Chain, Chain, Chain," which she performed with the Mo-town group the Funk Brothers. 

Family Life

Her father, Mike Jones, was a former NFL football player who played for several teams. She sang the National Anthem for multiple NFL games. 

Associated With

She once appeared on a Most Talented Kids episode of the Maury Povich Show.
