Adventure Weather: Tubing hills in Idaho

Snow is finally starting to land in the Treasure Valley, and tubing is a fun way to enjoy the shift in weather.

Here are some places to go tubing in Idaho:

Gateway Parks at Eagle Island State Park – Eagle

Bogus Basin Tubing Hill – Bogus Basin

Steamboat Gulch – Idaho City

McCall Activity Barn – McCall

Penny Hill - Sun Valley
