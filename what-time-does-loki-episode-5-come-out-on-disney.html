What Time Does Loki Episode 5 Come Out on Disney+?

With the finale in sight, Loki is just absolutely positively doing the most. Last week’s episode was a complete game-changer for the entire series. That episode, titled “The Nexus Event,” gave us a glimpse at Sylvie’s origin, revealed the true nature of the Time Keepers, showed us Ravonna Renslayer’s shady side, and dropped our fave time-displaced Loki into what looks to be a land of Lokis. We have no idea what’s going on, but we’re definitely gonna find out!

So, how much longer do you have to wait for Loki’s return to Disney+? Here’s everything you need to know about Loki Episode 5 on Disney+.

What is the Loki Episode 5 release date?

There’s another all-new episode of Loki dropping on Disney+ on Wednesday, July 7.

What time does Loki come out on Disney+?

New episodes of Loki pop up on Disney+ on Wednesdays at 3:01 a.m. ET / 12:01 a.m. PT. Whether or not you stay up stupid late on Tuesday night and into Wednesday morning is your call. And if you decide to watch Loki episode 5 in the evening rather than first thing in the morning, be sure to keep a look out for spoilers! Well, not a literal look out. You don’t want to see them.

When will the Loki finale come out on Disney+?

Loki’s wild adventure will come screeching to a halt with the release of Episode 6 on Wednesday, July 14. Is that worth taking a personal day from work in order to recover? Maybe!

When will new episodes of Loki come out on Disney+? Loki episode count

Instead of dropping a whole season at once, Loki will release episodes week by week. Loki’s release schedule looks like:

  • Episode 5: Wednesday, July 7, 2021
  • Episode 6: Wednesday, July 14, 2021

New episodes of Loki premiere on Wednesdays on Disney+.
