NOPD homicide detective charged with driving drunk

NEW ORLEANS (WVUE) - A New Orleans Police Department homicide detective was booked into jail on suspicion of driving under the influence.

At the time of the incident, Darling was off duty and was operating her personal vehicle, the NOPD said.
