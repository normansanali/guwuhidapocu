Matt Rife Bio (Wiki)

Who is Matt Rife?

Matt Rife is a standup comedian and actor.

He is best known for his appearance on MTV’s comedy series, Wild ‘N Out, seven and nine series, as a leading cast member.

He is famous for a recurring performance in the World Famous Laugh Factory.

Matt Rife- Career

Matt Rife started his career as a stand-up comedian at the age of 15.

In the process of his journey, he performed with various other huge comedian stars such as Dane Cook, Finesse Mitchell, Deray Davis, Ralphie May, Mike Epps, Erik Griffin, etc.

Matt Rife became one of the youngest comedians and recurring performers at the World Famous Laugh Factory. Laugh Factory is the biggest chain comedy club in Hollywood.

In 2014, he debut in the comedy Tv-series, Average Joe.

Matt appeared in seasons 7 and 9 of the MTV comedy Reality show, Wild ‘N OUT, in 2016.

In 2016, he starred in the comedy Tv Series, WTH: Welcome to Howler.

In addition, he played in several series and TV movies such as Brooklyn Nine-Nine, American Typecast, Stalked by My Doctor: A Sleepwalker’s Nightmare, and Fresh Off the Boat.
