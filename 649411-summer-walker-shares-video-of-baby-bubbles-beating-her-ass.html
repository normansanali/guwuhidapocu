Summer Walker Shares Video Of Baby Bubbles &quot;Beating [Her] Ass&quot;

Her relationship with her baby daddies may not be exactly what she expected. Still, Summer Walker always knew she wanted to be a mother of multiple children at a relatively young age. She and London on da Track share her oldest, known as Bubbles. Of course, they've had a difficult time publicly navigating their co-parenting situation. When the Still Over It hitmaker announced her second pregnancy, this one with then-boyfriend Larry, fans were overjoyed that she had a shot at experiencing a healthy romance while carrying her babies.

As her due date neared closer last year, internet sleuths noticed Walker's beau's sudden absence from her social media posts. They accordingly began speculating that trouble was afoot for the pair. She denied the gossip at first. Later, though, the mother of three later confirmed that she and Larry decided to go their separate ways. While the news was undeniably heartbreaking, she's managed to soldier on with grace as she adjusts to life with two more children.
