Olivia Buckland Bio, Parents, Husband, Children

Olivia Buckland Bio, Parents, Husband, Children: Olivia Buckland, also known as Olivia Bowen is an English reality television personality, influencer, and author born on January 3, 1994, in Chelmsford, United Kingdom.

She’s famous for her role on the second series of the popular dating show ‘Love Island’ in 2016. After Love Island, Olivia appeared on several other reality shows, including; Celebrity Island With Bear Grylls and The Only Way Is Essex, among others.

She has also been a brand ambassador for various companies and has endorsed products on her social media platforms. In addition to her reality TV career, she’s also an influencer with over 1 million followers on Instagram where she often shares pictures of her personal life, as well as modeling work.
