Jennifer Lopez's Backup Dancer Suffers Wardrobe Malfunction at AMAs

Oops! Jennifer Lopez‘s opening number at the 2015 American Music Awards on Sunday, Nov. 22, was bootylicious — perhaps a little more so than planned. Toward the end of the energetic performance, one of J.Lo’s many backup dancers suffered a wardrobe malfunction, exposing her butt to the audience for a split second.

Lopez, 46, who hosted the night’s festivities, got things going with an epic dance number set to a medley of the year’s hottest songs, including Nicki Minaj‘s “Anaconda,” The Weeknd‘s “Can’t Feel My Face,” and Drake‘s “Hotline Bling.” She and all of her female dancers wore skintight nude bodysuits decorated to look like tribal tattoos, while the men wore tribal tattoo tops tucked into jeans.

In the final minute of the opening, one member of the troupe split open the seat of her catsuit. The dancers appeared to be wearing nude leotards underneath, but viewers nonetheless took note of the malfunction.

The dancer, for her part, handled the moment like a total pro and didn’t miss a single beat as she closed out the performance with Lopez. Fortunately for her, most of the final moves had her facing the audience.
