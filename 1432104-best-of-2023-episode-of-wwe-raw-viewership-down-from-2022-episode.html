Best Of 2023 Episode Of WWE RAW Viewership Down From 2022 Episode

WWE RAW viewership dropped with the “Best of” episode on December 25.

The Christmas night edition of RAW was a “Best Of” show. Wrestlenomics reports that an average of 698,000 viewers tuned in. This number is down from the 2022 “Best of” episode, which averaged 1.74 million viewers. Monday’s number was a new all-time low for RAW.

In the 18 to 49 demographic, RAW scored a 0.18 rating, which is also down from last year (0.27 rating in the same demographic).

WrestleZone will have coverage of the “Day 1” episode of WWE RAW on January 1.
