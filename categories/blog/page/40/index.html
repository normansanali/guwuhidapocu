Can you take a lunch to the Ark Encounter?

published on September 25, 2024
Yes, visitors are allowed to bring their own lunch to the Ark Encounter. The park understands that guests may prefer to bring their own meals for various reasons, such as dietary restrictions or personal preferences. However, please note that outside food and drinks are not allowed in the restaurants and cafes within the Ark Encounter. There are designated picnic areas available where visitors can enjoy their packed lunches.
