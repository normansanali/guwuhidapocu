Is Dylan OBrien Gay?

Dylan Rhodes O’Brien, an American actor was born August 26, 1991 in New York City, United States. His first major role was Stiles Stilinski on the MTV supernatural drama Teen Wolf.
Since then, he has featured in movies like Maze Runner(2014–2018), American Assassin (2017), Bumblebee (2018), Love and Monsters (2020) and others.

O’Brien is the son of Lisa, a former actress who managed an acting school, and Patrick O’Brien, a camera operator. He grew up in Springfield Township, New Jersey, until he and his family relocated to Hermosa Beach, California, when he was twelve years old. He is descended from Irish, Italian, English, and Spanish ancestors. He pondered studying sports broadcasting and potentially working for the New York Mets after graduating from Mira Costa High School in 2009.

Is Dylan O’Brien Gay?

Dylan O’Brien has not made any public statements about his sexuality. However he has dated several females. There is no information as to whether he is gay or not.
