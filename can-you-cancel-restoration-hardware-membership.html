Furthermore, what is a Restoration Hardware membership?Restoration Hardware (RH) thinks it’s found a way to wean customers off their addiction to discounts: a subscription service. Restoration Hardware rarely offers free shipping promos online. But when you place your order in the store, certain items ship free. The free shipping doesn’t apply to big pieces like furniture and lighting, but smaller items such as hardware, towels and some bedding ship free.
