WWE 2K24 Confirms Inclusion of Boxing Legend Muhammad Ali

WWE 2K24 is already upon us and the game will be bringing a ton of new additions for fans to enjoy. This includes real-life WWE referees and other things being part of the game and now even Muhammad Ali has been confirmed to be part of the game as well.

Fans were eagerly anticipating the announcement of the cover star for WWE 2K24. The excitement reached its peak when it was revealed that Cody Rhodes would grace the cover of the game this year.

WWE 2K games also introduced a ton of new options for fans and game modes as well, including Special Guest Referee. In addition to that, the game has a ton of other things that fans will enjoy.

2K Games dropped their first official proper gameplay trailer for WWE 2K24, showing off a bevvy of features and enhancements. They also seemingly saved the best for last, providing a glimpse of Muhammad Ali in the game.

In addition, it has been revealed that WWE 2K24 is set to be released on March 8th, 2024. The response from both fans and critics is yet to be determined, but the anticipation surrounding the game is exceptionally high.
