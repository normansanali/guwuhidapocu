How many title defenses does Joanna Jedrzejczyk have?

Joanna Jedrzejczyk, often hailed as the greatest female strawweight mixed martial artist of all time, has had an illustrious career in the world of mixed martial arts, particularly in the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC).

Throughout her career, Joanna Jedrzejczyk has achieved remarkable success, including multiple title defenses in the UFC Women's Strawweight Championship. 

The Early Career and UFC Debut

Joanna Jedrzejczyk's foray into mixed martial arts commenced in May 2012, and she swiftly rose through the ranks, remaining undefeated in her first nine fights.

Her remarkable skills in Muay Thai kickboxing translated seamlessly into the world of MMA, leading to her UFC Women's Strawweight Championship victory in March 2015 after defeating Carla Esparza at UFC 185.

Unprecedented Success: Title Defenses and Records

Joanna Jedrzejczyk's reign as the UFC Women's Strawweight Champion witnessed unparalleled success, marked by a series of impressive title defenses.

Notably, she holds several UFC records in the strawweight division, including the most successful strawweight title defenses and the most consecutive wins at strawweight.

  • Karolina Kowalkiewicz - UFC 205: Alvarez vs McGregor (Nov. 12, 2016):

    • Jedrzejczyk defended her title for the fourth consecutive time by defeating Kowalkiewicz by unanimous decision.

  • Jéssica Andrade - UFC 211: Miocic vs. Dos Santos (May. 13, 2017):

    • She won the fight via unanimous decision, landing the most significant strikes in UFC championship history and breaking her previous record for most leg kicks thrown in a single fight.

    • The above title defenses showcase Jedrzejczyk's dominance and resilience as a champion, solidifying her reputation as a force to be reckoned with in the strawweight division of the UFC.

    Notable Bouts and Legacy

    Despite her remarkable string of victories, Jedrzejczyk suffered her first professional loss in a bout against Rose Namajunas at UFC 217 on November 4, 2017.

    This marked a turning point in her career, as she faced subsequent challenges and victories in her journey as a professional fighter.

    Joanna Jedrzejczyk's legacy in mixed martial arts, particularly in the UFC Women's Strawweight Championship, is defined by her exceptional skill, resilience, and remarkable achievements, including her record-setting title defenses.

     

    Joanna Jedrzejczyk's remarkable career has left an indelible mark on the world of mixed martial arts, and her contributions to the sport will be celebrated for generations to come.
