Angie Goff is the Korean born American broadcast journalist popular as the content creator and host of the popular podcast The Oh MY Goff Show. Goff also serves for Fox owned and operated Television Station WTTG, also known as Fox 5.

Angie was born as Anglea Goff on March 17, 1980, in Seoul, South Korea, under the zodiac sign of Pisces. She is the daughter of the parent's Chin Goff (mother) and Ren Goff (father). Angie has two sisters Jenny Goff Phillips and Knotty Origami.

Concerning education, Angie attended Seoul American High School. She then graduated from Herndon High School. Afterward, she earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Arts from George Mason University.
