Rivington Starchild Net Worth

Rivington Starchild sits on a net worth above $500 thousand. Currently, Rivington is working as a fashion designer but before that, he was a musician and frontman to the band named New York rock band named DAMEHT.

DAMEHT was a three-man band, Rivington in the vocals, Roman Lewis (drums), and Lucas Garzoli (Guitar). Talking about the band’s name with Down Town 500 Magazine, they said, “DAMEHT itself came as a symbol to us, but it’s THE MAD backward.” The band also collaborated with photographer Clayton Patterson (who famously documented the Tompkins Square Park Riot of 1988).

Speaking with Nylon the band talked about why they collaborated with Clayton. They said, “A lot of the figures we’ve followed have always wanted to preserve something, whether that’s preserving love in poetry, film or music, or preserving the human spirit. In our case, we’re wanting to preserve innocence—the feeling of naivety that allows you to get lost in what you’re doing, whether that’s making a cap or writing a song.”

They added, “That’s what Clayton did during the period he was making caps. He has done visually what we’re doing sonically, although we’re adding a whole pop perspective. We were looking for ways to communicate concepts beyond our sound and he really captivated us, like a virus. Why create something new if someone’s already done it so big? Why not attach and make it bigger?”

They also collaborated with pop producer RedOne (who’s responsible for Lady Gaga’s biggest chart scorchers).

Moreover, they collaborated with Shoemaker George Cox to release the iconic creeper. As for their music, DAMEHT released Girl Jeans EP followed by a single Creeper Creeper inspired by the iconic British bootmaker. You can find more of their music on YouTube.

After moving to LA, he dropped music and delved into fashion. About his transition, he revealed to Union Los Angeles, “I no longer had the ability to make everyone feel involved. I was the lead singer who lost his ability to lead. People don’t have ideas, ideas have people, and the idea of the rockstar had taken hold of me, I’m glad I peeped through the curtains and saw through the act. Right around the time, all this was happening, I found out about the possible Union x Jordan 1’s drop… crazy to say but that was what got me interested in starting a brand.”

Rivington is the owner of Rivington roi Rebis (RRR123). He said, “The Rebis is the outcome of the great work in Alchemy, the merging of opposites. For example, Cuba (1) + Rivington (2) = The Rebis (3), a third entity. RIVINGTON roi Rebis, Rivington the King of Opposites. 123 is the monad, split into duality and the outcome of that duality makes the trinity. It’s the way we make decisions, one situation, two ways we can judge or go about it, and the third is the action we end up taking. RRR123 RIVINGTON roi Rebis.”
