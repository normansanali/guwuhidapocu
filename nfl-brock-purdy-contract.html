Brock Purdy Contract: How much money will the 49ers new sensational QB make in 2023?

The NFL has many players who are yet to showcase their talent on the field, one such being Brock Purdy, who is a very young and skilled player drafted by the San Francisco 49ers in the position of quarterback.

He was named the Gatorade Football Player of the year and even the High school football Player of the Year by the Arizona Republic after finishing his high school career with a record of 27-13.

Later, he committed to Iowa State University in 2018 and excelled in college, propelling his team to many wins. Following his time in college football, the San Francisco 49ers selected Brock Purdy as the 262nd overall pick in the 2022 draft. He made his professional debut as a backup for the starting QB Jimmy Garoppolo of the 49ers in the fourth quarter against Kansas City, tossing 66 yards as a new kid on the block.

On December 4, he was called back again in place of Garoppolo because of the veteran’s untimely foot injury while playing against the Miami Dolphins. Purdy, after the match, was selected as the 49ers’ starting quarterback for the remainder of the 2022 season.

In his most recent game, Purdy led the San Francisco 49ers to a stunning victory over the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, in the process defeating his idol and ‘GOAT’ Tom Brady. It would appear that Purdy is well on his way to becoming a legend soon.

Brock Purdy’s Contract details

The San Francisco 49ers rookie signed a four-year contract worth $3,737,008 that included a $77,008 signing bonus and an average salary of $934,252. According to Spotrac, Purdy will also receive a base salary of $705,000 in 2022, in addition to a signing bonus of $77,008, which is also his guaranteed money. In addition, the year 2023 will be giving him a salary of $870,000.

Brock Purdy’s net worth for the year 2022 is around $5 Million, which is indeed a good figure for a rookie player.

He will get more money from his salaries and even bonuses every season he plays. He is a young player with a lot of visible passion. With the kind of talent he has showcased in his limited games, analysts are already betting on him to be the first rookie QB who will win the Super Bowl.
