Jay-Z, Kendrick Lamar, Lil Nas X, J. Cole, Cardi B, others answer the question: Who are you?

John Ruskin, better known as Nardwuar the Human Serviette, is a Canadian interviewer and musician from Vancouver, British Columbia. 

In a clip compilation, popular music stars like Jay-Z, Cardi B, J. Cole, Future, Lil Wayne, Drake and many more answer the question ‘Who Are You’ as posed to them by Nardwuar.

A calmer Jay-Z holding tobacco goes like he is a young man living the American dream because there are a lot of skilled people who come from where he came from but are have not gotten where he is right now.

American rapper, Darryl McDaniels, better known by his stage name DMC also wax lyrical as he breaks down the meaning of his name and why people prefer to call him DMC. He says DMC means Darell Makes Cash.

Most of the artists were seen making cameo appearances by just mentioning their names.
