Biography

Alex Vincent was born in Newark, NJ on April 29, 1981. Former child actor best remembered as Andy Barclay in the 1988 horror classic Child’s Play and its 1990 sequel Child’s Play 2. In 2013, he reprised his role in Curse of Chucky. He worked with Brad Dourif, who provided the voice of Chucky in the Child’s Play horror films. He gained an interest in music after his child acting career ended and worked as a sound engineer.
