What is Joy Behar's salary on The View and net worth?

published on July 03, 2024
Joy Behar was born on October 7, 1942, in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, New York, Gino Occhiuto, her father, was a truck driver for the Coca Cola Company, and her mother, Rose Occhiuto née Carbone, was a seamstress. She was a part of ABC’s The View original roster for 16 years before returning as co-host in September 2015 for the show’s three most recent seasons. Behar is the recipient of three MAC awards, a CableACE award, and a 2000 GLAAD award for her longstanding connection with the LGBT community, in addition to the 2009 Daytime Emmy® Award for Outstanding Talk Show Host.
