Crazy In Love: Meet Mookie, Chrisean Rock Brother

Mookie, the older brother of Blueface’s girlfriend Chrisean Rock has also appeared on Zeus Network’s show Crazy In Love.

Although Mookie isn’t as popular as his sister, he has also shared his take on the couple’s relationship previously. In August 2022, Its_onsite posted on their IG. They wrote, “Wow, Chrisean Rock’s older brother speaks out about Chrisean’s and Blueface’s toxic, abusive relationship. He says that he is hurting for his sister and that he refuses to just sit back and watch Blueface continuously humiliate her over the internet. Swipe.”

He wrote, “Chrisean I Love You so much, and over here hurting bout you, I don’t say nothing wasn’t goin to until u introduced us in person cause the sh*t he be doin on the internet to you c’mon Chrisean. I grew up in and out of prison, I wouldn’t mine throwing all my freedom away forever for u.”

Mookie added, “You my baby sister, u our baby sister, Our Father raised us to protect our sisters at all costs, u da youngest one, I know you Love Blue, I just wanna ask him do he Love you? I can’t play bout u no more Chrisean, I’ll see you soon.”

In December 2022, Fox Baltimore reported that Mookie was arrested for first-degree attempted murder. Additionally, police confirmed that Mookie was taken into custody in connection with a shooting that occurred on October 10. It was around 8:45 p.m. that day in Baltimore when officers “were called to investigate a shot spotter alert.”

Eventually, when they arrived at the scene, it was stated that they found “evidence of a shooting.” Not long after, police were also told that a shooting victim had just walked into a hospital to be treated for injuries. The investigation led police to Mookie, but it is unclear how he is tied to the 39-year-old victim. Chrisean’s brother was reportedly charged with first-degree attempted murder.

Mookie had also recently called out Chrisean and Blueface, claiming they’re “the brokest celebrities in Hollywood.

“Y’all the brokest celebrities in Hollywood and the most lit right now. I manage y’all cause y’all not taking advantage of this lit sh*t and getting some money with it. Y’all broke as sh*t, stop playing Chrisean. You know what’s up with me, I’m back, I take care of everybody when I’m home,” Mookie said in an Instagram Live video. “You don’t get no motion, you got all that motion and don’t know what to do with it. Chrisean doesn’t play with me. I’ll see you on the first.”

Moreover, there isn’t much about their mom’s job and education. According to Baltimore Sun, in January 1997, police arrested Eugene and Charla during a raid on their Cape St. Claire home and charged them with stealing mail from neighbors.

Police raided the home at about 12: 30 p.m. after receiving information from area businesses about attempts to cash stolen checks. Police said they recovered a large amount of mail and checks stolen from mailboxes in the Cape St. Clair area. Some of the checks had been mailed to residents, and others had been left in the boxes for pickup.
