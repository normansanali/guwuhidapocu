Kendall Jenner, Harry Styles Go Skiing Together Over Holidays: Picture

Romancing from coast to coast! Kendall Jenner and Harry Styles didn't let the holidays slip away without seeing each other. The two were spotted hitting the slopes together in Mammoth, Calif., on Thursday, Jan. 2.

During the getaway, Jenner, 18, and Styles, 19, (decked out in Volcom gear) were spotted with a group of friends while gearing up to ski. The Keeping Up With the Kardashians reality star led the way in a black coat, grey pants, and with her brunette locks down in a braid. The One Direction singer, meanwhile, was all smiles as he bundled up in a matching black coat, and kept his face concealed with large ski goggles. (Just two days earlier, Jenner's sisters Kim and Kourtney Kardashian were photographed skiing with Kanye West and Scott Disick on New Year's Eve in Park City, Utah.)
