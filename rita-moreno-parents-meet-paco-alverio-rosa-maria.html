Rita Moreno, born Rosa Dolores Alverío Marcano, is a Puerto Rican actress, dancer, activist, and singer. Rita Moreno has been in the entertainment industry for decades.

Rita Moreno was born on December 11, 1931, in Humacao, Puerto Rico, and was married to her late husband, Leonard Gordon. They married from 1965 to 2010 after his demise. Her late husband was a cardiologist and also her manager. Rita Moreno has a child called Fernanda Luisa Gordon. Leonard Gordon was born on March 1920 in New York City.

She is currently in her 90s and has assumed challenging roles that have made her more famous. Rita Moreno has two grandchildren, Cameron Fisher, and Justin Fisher. She is one of the last remaining stars from the Golden Age of Hollywood.

Rita Moreno Parents: Meet Paco Alverío, Rosa María

She grew up in the villages of Valley Stream on Long Island, part of the town of Hempstead bordering New York City, without her brother. Rita Moreno’s mother left Paco” Alverío to marry Edward Moreno because of marital issues.
