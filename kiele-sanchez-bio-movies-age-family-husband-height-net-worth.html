Kiele Sanchez Biography

Kiele Sanchez is an American actress who is famous for starring in the A&E Network drama The Glades. Prior to her starring role in The Glades, she starred as Anne Sorelli in The WB comedy-drama Related, and as Nikki Fernandez on the main cast in thors season of the ABC television drama series Lost. In addition, she starred in the DirectTV drama series Kingdom on the Audience Network.

Born in Chicago, she began her acting career at Glenbard North High School in Carol Stream, Illinois. At this school, she performed in a school production of The Grape of Wrath. This was one of the things that helped her overcome her stage fright, which was one of the challenges she was facing at the time.

In 2000, she auditioned for MTV’s Wanna Be a VJ but did not win the competition. She later auditioned and got roles in the short-lived drama series That Was Then and the sitcom Married to the Kellys. In 2003, she appeared in Stuck on You and had a recurring role on the ABC show Samantha Who?

In 2010 she starred in the film 30 Days of Night: Dark Days, taking over the role of Stella Oleson from Melissa George. She also starred in the DirecTV drama series Kingdom. The series premiered on the Audience Network in the fall of 2014. Recently, in February 2020, she was cast as Lorna on the CBS series The Lincoln Lawyer.
