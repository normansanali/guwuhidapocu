Bill Bellamy Bio (Wiki)

Who Is Bill Bellamy?

Bill Bellamy is an American actor and stand-up comedian. He is popularly known for appearing in HBO’s Russell Simmons’ Def Comedy Jam and coining the phrase, “booty call.”. He is also known for playing the role of Jimmy Sanderson in the American sports drama film Any Given Sunday.

Bill Bellamy- Bio, Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Bill Bellamy was born in Newark, New Jersey on April 7, 1965. His birth name is William Bellamy. As of 2023, he is 58 years old. He is American by nationality and his ethnicity is Afro-Amriacan. Similarly, his zodiac sign is Aries.

Talking about his parents, he was born to Edna Bellamy (mother) and William Bellamy (father). Similarly, he has a sister named Karen Bellamy and a brother Julius Jermaine Bellamy. He is also the cousin brother of basketball player Shaquille O’Neal.

Speaking about her educational background, she attended the Seton Hall Preparatory School. Later, she studied economics at Rutgers University.

Bill Bellamy-Professional Life, Careers

Talking about his career, Bill Bellamy has been active in the entertainment industry since 1985. He has played in several movies and television series till now. Bill worked on MTV, was a VJ, and was the host of a number of MTV programs including MTV Jamz and MTV Beach House for many years.

Moreover, he made his television appearance as Antique Dealer in the British television drama anthology series, The Play on One. In 1992, he made his appearance in the HBO television series Def Comedy Jam which was produced by Russell Simmons and Stan Lathan.

In 1993, he made his film debut playing the role of Ace Man in the 1993 American romance film Joey Breaker along with Richard Edson and Cedella Marley.

Furthermore, in 1999, he played the role of Jimmy Sanderson in the American sports drama film Any Given Sunday which was directed by Oliver Stone. Similarly, he also appeared as host for seasons 5 and 6 of NBC’s Last Comic Standing reality show.

In 2014, he made his guest appearance on the TV Land original series Hot in Cleveland as Councilman Powell. Later, he was seen as  FBI Agent Sanders in Kindergarten Cop 2 in 2016.

His other film credit includes:
