Anna Nicole Smiths Daughter Dannielynn Birkhead Wears Janet Jacksons Exact Outfit to Kentucky

Dannielynn Birkhead is paying tribute to Janet Jackson!

On Friday night (May 6), the 15-year-old daughter of the late Anna Nicole Smith attended the 2022 Barnstable Brown Kentucky Derby Eve Gala with her dad Larry Birkhead at the Barnstable-Brown Mansion in Louisville, Kentucky.

For the event, Dannielynn wore the exact same outfit Janet, 55, wore to that event back in 2003.

Click inside to read more…

While they were inside the event, Dannielynn got to meet Janet!

“What a night! Dannielynn got to meet @janetjackson at the Barnstable-Brown Gala, while wearing Janet’s outfit that she wore to the same party in 2003. Janet was so gracious and complimentary of Dannielynn in her outfit. She made Dannielynn so happy and even graciously posed for a photo with us,” Larry, 49, wrote on Instagram along with photos of Dannielynn and Janet.

Each year, Dannielynn and Larry attend the Kentucky Derby, which is where Larry and Anna first met in 2003.
