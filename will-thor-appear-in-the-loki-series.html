Will Thor appear in the Loki series?

TOM Hiddleston stars as the title character in the much-anticipated Disney+ series Loki, which launched on June 9, 2021.

The six-episode run features an All-Star cast, but fans are buzzing about the possibility of Thor’s Chris Hemsworth making an appearance.

Will Thor appear in the Loki series?

Squabbling siblings Loki and Thor have appeared together in six Marvel Universe films, and there has been speculation that they could share the screen in the new streaming series.

Hemsworth is not listed among the cast for Loki’s IMDb page, but that would not prevent Thor from making a surprise appearance.

“Most plot details about Loki haven’t been revealed, but the trailers have shown many bits of his travels across time and space, along with footage from previous Marvel movies and Loki repeating some of his most famous quotes,” according to Screen Rant.

“All this has raised the question of whether Thor will make an appearance in the show or not … “

Thor and Loki first appeared together in 2011’s Thor – which featured Hiddleston as the main villain.

Hiddleston’s Loki was killed off in the early stages of 2018’s Avengers: Infinity War, but a version of the God of Mischief resurfaced in an alternate timeline in Avengers: Endgame one year later.

The streaming series Loki follows the events that took place in Endgame.

The alternate version of Loki is brought to the Time Variance Authority, which regulates the timeline.

Loki is given a choice: face being erased from existence or help fix the timeline.
