How many businesses does Chris Hemsworth own?

The actor is best known as Marvel’s Thor, but after recently selling his fitness app Centr for more than $200 million, Hemsworth’s financial influence should not be underestimated. In fact, his earnings from his business activities far outnumber those from his Hollywood career.

In 2019, Hemsworth launched Centr as an expert-driven fitness, nutrition, and mindfulness platform, and he was able to rapidly increase the value of the membership service. It provides daily planners, workout programs, meal planning, nutritional advice, and guided meditations to help you build a healthy body and mind.

HighPost Capital bought the company in March 2022, at the same time it bought exercise equipment firm Inspire, with the intention of combining their expertise. As the combined company’s second-largest stakeholder, Hemsworth will remain deeply involved in all parts of Centr.

Hemsworth spoke about the takeover and how it will help the company expand in the future. “I look forward to partnering with HighPost and Inspire to continue the success of Centr, inspire people to lead healthier and more active lives, and increase our strong subscriber base worldwide by developing outstanding new content and expanding our offerings,” he said.

“Centr was created to provide people with carefully curated and integrated fitness, nutrition, and mindfulness programs, guiding beginners and more experienced users in achieving their unique health and fitness goals.”
