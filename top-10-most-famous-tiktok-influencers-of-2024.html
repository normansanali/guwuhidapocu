Top 10 Most Famous Tiktok Influencers of 2024

TikTok, the fastest-growing social media app in the world, has catapulted numerous influencers to stardom. In 2024, the platform is dominated by a diverse group of immensely popular creators.

1. Khabane "Khaby" Lame - @KHABY.LAME (161.6M)

Khabane "Khaby" Lame, an Italian social media sensation originally from Senegal, is the most-followed user on TikTok.

Renowned for his humorous parodies of life hack videos, his rise to fame was marked by his comedic skits and reactions that resonated with a global audience.

Additionally, he was listed in Fortune's 40 Under 40 and Forbes' 30 Under 30 in 2022, solidifying his influence and impact in the social media landscape.

2. Charli D'Amelio - @CHARLIDAMELIO (151.8M)

Charli D'Amelio, the queen of TikTok, boasts a massive fan base and is renowned for her dance and lip-sync content. She was the first TikTok user to reach 100 million followers, a testament to her widespread appeal.

Charli has collaborated with major brands, appeared in music videos, and even started her own branded merchandise. 

3. Bella Poarch - @BELLAPOARCH (93.9M)

Bella Poarch gained massive TikTok fame with her viral lip-syncing video to Millie B’s “M to the B,” which became the most-liked TikTok in history.

Notably, she has used her platform to advocate against anti-Asian prejudice, showcasing the significant impact influencers can have in addressing social issues.

4. Addison Rae - @ADDISONRE (88.3M)

Addison Rae Easterling, renowned for her dance and lip-sync videos, has not only secured brand partnerships but also ventured into acting roles.

Her influence extends to various spheres, and her ability to engage with diverse audiences has solidified her position as a prominent figure in the TikTok community.

5. Zach King - @ZACHKING (80.6M)

Zach King, known for his illusionist videos, has captivated audiences with his innovative and creative content. His mastery of visual effects and illusions has garnered him a significant following on TikTok, showcasing the power of creativity and storytelling on the platform.

6. Spencer Polanco Knight - @SPENCERX (54.9M)

Spencer Polanco Knight, famous for beatboxing, has not only showcased his musical talents but also collaborated with renowned artists, showcasing the collaborative nature of TikTok influencers in expanding their reach and influence.

7. Loren Gray - @LORENGRAY (51.4M)

Loren Gray, a singer and influencer, has leveraged her platform to secure partnership deals with brands and launch her own jewelry brand.

8. Dixie D'Amelio - @DIXIEDAMELIO (49.4M)

Dixie D’Amelio, a singer and influencer, has not only secured sponsorship deals from top brands but also ventured into acting and music, showcasing her versatility and influence in various entertainment spheres.

9. Michael Le - @JUSTMAIKO (45.6M)

Michael Le, known as JustMaiko, has gained popularity for his exceptional dance skills and captivating choreography.

His collaborations with various other dancers and personalities have expanded his influence and showcased the collaborative nature of TikTok influencers in elevating their content.

10. Riyaz Aly - @RIYAZ.14 (43.9M)

Riyaz Aly, an actor and model, has gained a massive TikTok fanbase with his Bollywood-style lip-syncing and dance videos.

His influence extends beyond TikTok, as he has been featured in multiple hit music videos, showcasing the potential for TikTok influencers to transcend the platform and expand their influence.

 

All in all, the diverse and influential nature of these TikTok influencers in 2024 exemplifies the significant impact they have on the platform and beyond.
