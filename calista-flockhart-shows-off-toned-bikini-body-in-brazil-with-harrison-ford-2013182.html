Calista Flockhart Shows Off Toned Bikini Body in Brazil With Harrison Ford

Calista Flockhart looks tanned and toned! The 48-year-old Brothers & Sisters alum showed off her fit figure while vacationing in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, on Feb. 17.

Wearing a black bikini top and blue swim shorts, Flockhart was all smiles as her family relaxed at the world-famous Ipanema beach. The Ally McBeal star is visiting the South American country with Harrison Ford and their 12-year-old son, Liam.

The trip comes just four years after Ford surprised Flockhart with a Valentine’s Day proposal. The couple, who met at the 2002 Golden Globes, tied the knot in New Mexico in June, 2010. “Harrison is a wonderful parent,” Flockhart once told Red magazine of the Indiana Jones star’s parenting skills. “He’s funny and warm and loving.”

Despite their vast age difference, Ford has high hopes for his future with Flockhart. “I was 60 when I fell in love with Calista,” he told Hello! in 2009. “What surprised me most was that I was still capable of establishing a long-lasting relationship, making a serious commitment. With my children, in my private life or my work, I try to learn from my mistakes and evolve.”
