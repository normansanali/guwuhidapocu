Wiki/Biography

David Birtwistle was born on Thursday, 30 March 1990 (age 30 years; as of 2020) in London, England, UK. He attended St Mary’s University, Twickenham to pursue Bachelors in Strength and Conditioning Science. In 2013, he attended London South Bank University to get a foundation degree in Engineering. Following this, he graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Petroleum Engineering. He has a BTN Nutrition Academy Qualification, and also a Diploma in Elite Athlete Development (rugby).

Career

After finishing his graduation, in 2016, David joined MRC Mineral Commodities in South Africa, where he worked for a month. He quit his job and began working as a fitness and nutrition coach. Soon, he opened his own fitness studio, “David Coaching,” in 2011. In 2015, he became a head coach at F45 Training, an international fitness community. In 2019, he started his own company called ‘Endeavour Life.’
